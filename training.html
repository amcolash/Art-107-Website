Best Workout for Fast Legs

September, 15, 2014 @ 3:27 PM


Although I am a biker at my core, I find that running sprints can really help me on longer biking sprints. One of the best running workout that I have done is as follows, good luck!


On a track, do the following 2 times for an insane workout and once for a very hard one:

  1. 800m warmup
  2. 400m sprint
  3. 100m walk
  4. 300m sprint
  5. 50m walk
  6. 250m sprint
  7. 25m walk
  8. 200m sprint
  9. 50m walk
  10. 150m srpint
  11. 25m walk
  12. 100m sprint
  13. 25m walk
  14. 50m sprint
  15. 400m cooldown


Back when I was in cross country, a coach of mine made me do this. I recently did it when I remembered about it, I will never truly forget that workout - it is brutal. It is one of the best things you can do for you muscles to work them thoroughly as well as making them quick.
